About Us

PickandPlay.com was created to help beginning guitarists like you, well...pick up and play the guitar! We've all been there, hearing our favorite songs and wanting to play them, but not knowing where to start. The resorces on this web site will be tailor-made for budding guitarists like you to help you get playing the guitar as soon as possible.
