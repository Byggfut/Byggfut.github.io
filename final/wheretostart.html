So You Want to Play Guitar?

Awesome! Let's start by going over what you Need to Know

Tools to Start

  • A guitar (electric or acosutic will work)
  • Picks (Any Thickness will do, but between .6 mm and 1mm is recommended)
  • A sturdy chair (please, save yourself the trouble and try to find one that doesn't have arms)
  • A tuner. While a dedicated tuner would be optimal, there are a number of mobile phone apps that, while not perfect, work admirably.
  • (Optional) a capo. This will be particlalrly useful if you would like to sing along as you play guita. We'll go into more detail later.

Compenents of the guitar

Posture

  • Posture can have a tremendous impact on your ability to play guitar
  • Start by finding a chair without arms. If you try playing with arms, you'll soon discover why this is so important. Chair arms tend to get in the way of proper guitar posture

How to Position the Guitar

1 / 3
Place the guitar on your right leg. There should be a slight indent near the cavity of the guitar that should fit perfectly within your leg
2 / 3
Let your arm hang over the top of the car, near the widest section
2 / 3
Place the bottom of your palm over the "bridge" of the guitar, keeping your fingers right above the strings
